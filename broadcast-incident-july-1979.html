Broadcast Incident Report

Date: July 31, 1979

Station: FairlyUSA 40.1 FM

Incident ID: BI-079-31

On the evening of July 31, 1979, FairlyUSA experienced an unscheduled interruption to its morning broadcast.

The interruption coincided with the absence of the assigned on-air announcer.

Station personnel initiated a temporary signal loop while attempting internal communication.

At approximately 10:34 local time, the announcer was found deceased within station premises.

The cause of death was later classified as suicide.

No emergency broadcast was issued. Programming resumed under automated control shortly thereafter.

Listeners reporting irregular audio or unauthorized speech are advised that no such transmission was logged.

INTERNAL NOTE: Refer all inquiries to administrative management.
